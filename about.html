About Sinful Sake

As an enthusiast of anime fanservice, I created this website to push anime fanservice to the limit and spreading it to other enthusiast and average enjoyers.

Fanservice in anime is looked down by the vast majority and gives anime on a whole a bad name as a result. I disagree with this notion and wished fanservice was more socially acceptable.

The goal is to find fanservice in all genres of anime and deliver it to my fellow fanservice enthusiast and enjoyers. Enjoy your time exploring and remember to take a sip of the Sinful Sake

Expect weekly reactions, full reviews, and character rankings that might offend you (and that’s okay).

This isn’t a professional critic site, this is just honest anime watching and enjoying.
