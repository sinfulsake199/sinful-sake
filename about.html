🧠 About Sinful Sake

Sinful Sake is where anime gets reviewed without sugarcoating.

If it’s peak, you’ll hear it. If it’s mid, I’ll say it. If it’s trash… yeah, it’s going in the bucket.

Expect weekly reactions, full reviews, and character rankings that might offend you (and that’s okay).

This isn’t a professional critic site—this is just honest anime watching.
