Amagi Brilliant Park Was Not What I Expected

Review • April 2026

🔥 Quick Verdict

This show surprised me in ways I didn’t expect… and not always in a good way.

🤔 Expectations vs Reality

Going into this, I thought I was getting a simple, lighthearted anime. What I got instead was something way more chaotic and weird.

✅ What Worked

  • Unique concept
  • Some genuinely funny moments
  • Characters had personality

❌ What Didn’t

  • Pacing felt off
  • Some jokes didn’t land
  • Could’ve done more with the story

🧍 Character Thoughts

The characters definitely carried parts of the show. Some of them? Yeah… they’re making it into Bucket of Bust eventually.

🏆 Final Rating

7 / 10

📊 Bucket Placement

Still deciding… but I already have a few suspects 👀
